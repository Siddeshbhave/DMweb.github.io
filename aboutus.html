ABOUT US




UPASS is a decade old Group with impeccable track record in Frugal automation for Real Time Transaction Management through
Mobility APPs.

From its humble beginnings in late 2008, UPASS has handled over 50 Crore Transactions with a cumulative Value of over Re.25,000 Crore. UPASS has had presence across diverse verticals such as BFSI, Telecom, Cable TV and Logistics.

Looking at the changing Market composition and the rapid pace of technology transformation, UPASS undertook a restructuring and repositioning exercise.

As a part thereof, it spun off some accounts and focused on creating versatile platforms.

At UPASS we believe that the Primary Technology Objectives are -
To Make Life Better, Enhance Customer Experience, Build Trust, Credibility, Revenue Assurance & Fraud Prevention.





Our Mission is to -
Uplift mundane Jobs into Careers through Horizontalized competencies
Reduce transaction time and costs without compromising quality and credibility And Share Technology Dividend across the entire Value Chain

An Individual with reasonable Education, Smart Device and Intelligent Technology
Platform to assist him is a powerhouse to deliver host of services and thereby find sustenance for self while adding Value to others
